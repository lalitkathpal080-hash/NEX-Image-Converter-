About NEX Hub Tools

NEX Hub Tools is a collection of free, browser-based utilities built for everyone. Whether you're a designer converting images, a developer making favicons, or just someone who needs to quickly change an image format — NEX has you covered.

Our biggest promise: your files never leave your device. Every tool runs entirely in your browser using modern Web APIs like Canvas, FileReader, and Blob. No server receives your data. No cloud stores your files. What happens in your browser, stays in your browser.
